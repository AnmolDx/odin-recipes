Butter Chicken

Discription

An easy butter chicken recipe I've perfected over the years and shared with many others. The sauce is perfect when creamy and not too thick or too thin. The level of salt and spices can be suited to your taste. Garam and tandoori masala can be found in the ethnic aisle of your grocery store.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken: Boneless cubes (thigh meat is best)
  • Marinade: ½ cup yogurt, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder, salt, and lemon juice
  • Gravy Base: 1 cup tomato puree, 1 onion (finely chopped), and 10-12 cashews (soaked and ground to paste)
  • Essentials: 3 tbsp butter, ½ cup heavy cream, 1 tbsp sugar/honey
  • Spices: 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp dried fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi)

Steps

  1. Marinate: Mix chicken with marinade ingredients. Let it rest for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  2. Cook Chicken: Pan-fry or grill the chicken pieces in a little oil until cooked through and slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. Make Gravy: In the same pan, melt butter. Sauté onions until soft, then add tomato puree and cashew paste. Cook until butter separates from the masala.
  4. Simmer: Add sugar, garam masala, and a splash of water. Simmer for 5 minutes. Add the cooked chicken.
  5. Finish: Stir in fresh cream and crushed dried fenugreek leaves (Kasuri Methi). Simmer for 2 minutes.
  6. Serve: Garnish with a swirl of cream and serve hot with Naan.
